Hi there! I'm a student pursuing a B.Sc. with a joint major of physics and computer science at McGill University in Montréal, Québec.
extracurriculars
I'm currently a part of the McGill Space Systems Group (MSSG) as a member of the competition team. My role is to act as a liaison between the software and communications subsystems.
research
I am currently working as a research assistant in the Gervais Group at McGill, working on LabGUI - software written in Python that interfaces with lab equipment.

Over the summer of 2017, I was a research assistant as part of the ATLAS group at McGill. My project was a contribution to the upgrade of the ATLAS detector at the CERN supercollider. In particular, I worked on implementing an optimal filter on an FPGA using VHDL, as well as some analysis on ATLAS data using CERN's ROOT framework. I authored a scientific poster with the results of my research, and gave a talk at the 53rd Canadian Undergraduate Physics Conference.
